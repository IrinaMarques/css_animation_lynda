Keyframes:
@keyframe *name* { from: {*starting point*;} to {*ending point*}
@keyframe *name* { % percentages { *animation*}


Properties:
animation-name: *name of the keyframe* ;
animation-duration: *time*;
animation-timing-function: *behave during the duration*;
animation-iteration-count: *times will play*;
animation-delay: *time before the animation actually executes*;
animation-fill-mode: *what to do outside the actual duration of its animation - none|backwards|forwards|both.*;
